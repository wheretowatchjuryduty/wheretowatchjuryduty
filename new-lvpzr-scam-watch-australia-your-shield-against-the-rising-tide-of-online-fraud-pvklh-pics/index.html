Understanding the Scope of the Problem:

Before examining Scam Watch’s contribution, it’s crucial to understand the scale of the problem. The ACCC’s annual reports consistently reveal staggering losses attributed to scams. Millions of dollars are lost annually by Australians across a broad spectrum of fraudulent activities. These losses aren’t just financial; they inflict significant emotional distress, erode trust, and can have devastating consequences for victims.
