1. Digital Rentals and Purchases:

This is likely the most accessible option for many viewers. Following its theatrical run, John Wick: Chapter 4 became available for digital rental and purchase through various platforms. These platforms offer a convenient way to watch the film at your own pace and on your preferred device. 2. Physical Media (Blu-ray and 4K Ultra HD Blu-ray):

For cinephiles who prefer the tangible experience of owning a physical copy, John Wick: Chapter 4 is available on Blu-ray and 4K Ultra HD Blu-ray. These formats offer superior picture and sound quality compared to streaming, making them ideal for multiple viewings and preserving the film for years to come.
